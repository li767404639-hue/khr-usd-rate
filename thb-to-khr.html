Why check THB to KHR first?

If you exchange Thai Baht in Cambodia, the quoted rate can vary between shops and services. Checking a reference rate first helps you estimate whether you are getting a fair conversion.

Tips before exchanging money

  • Compare the quoted rate with a reference rate before you exchange.
  • Check if any extra service fee is charged separately.
  • For larger THB amounts, small rate differences matter more.
  • Compare cash exchange options with transfer services if needed.
