Quick checklist

Bank-style tips

Total cost = fee + FX spread

Some services show low fees but use a worse exchange rate. Always compare the final received amount.

Confirm destination options

Delivery method matters (bank transfer / cash pickup / wallet). Availability varies by provider.

Know transfer time

Faster transfers may cost more. If time is flexible, you may reduce costs.
