Cash pickup or bank transfer?

Bank transfer

If the receiver has a Cambodian bank account such as ABA or ACLEDA, bank transfer is often cheaper and gives a better exchange rate.

Cash pickup

If the receiver does not have a bank account, services like Remitly or Western Union may be more practical because they support cash pickup.

Typical transfer differences

  • Wise usually has the best exchange rate margin for bank transfers.
  • Remitly is often more practical when cash pickup matters.
  • Western Union usually has broader pickup coverage, but fees can be higher.

Compare your options before sending money. Better exchange rates and lower fees can make a noticeable difference.

Frequently asked questions

Can I receive cash in Cambodia?
Yes. Some providers support cash pickup in Cambodia. This is usually more relevant for users without a bank account.
Which service usually has the best exchange rate?
Wise often gives one of the strongest exchange rates for bank transfers, but the best option depends on whether you need bank delivery or cash pickup.
Should I compare fees before sending money?
Yes. Even small differences in fees and exchange rates can change how much the receiver gets in Cambodia.
