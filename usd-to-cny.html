Why check USD to CNY first?

If you exchange US Dollars to Chinese Yuan, a small difference in exchange rate can affect the final amount you receive. Checking a reference rate first helps you compare offers more accurately.

Tips before exchanging money

  • Compare the quoted rate with a reference market rate.
  • Check whether service fees are included separately.
  • For larger USD amounts, even a small rate difference matters.
  • If needed, compare exchange options with transfer services as well.
