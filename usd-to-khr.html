Why check USD to KHR first?

A small difference in exchange rate can make a noticeable difference in the final amount received in Cambodian Riel. Before exchanging money or sending funds to Cambodia, it is a good idea to compare the rate you are offered with a reference market rate.

Tips before exchanging money

  • Compare the offered rate with a reference rate.
  • Check whether fees are included in the final amount.
  • For larger amounts, even a small rate difference matters.
  • International transfer services may offer better value than local exchange counters.

Need to send money to Cambodia?

If you are sending money internationally, compare transfer services before you decide. A better exchange rate and lower fees can increase the final amount received.
